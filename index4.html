List of Tag

  1. h1:A primary heading used for important page titles.
  2. h2:Subheading providing hierarchy and structure to content sections.
  3. p:HTML tag for structuring and displaying text content blocks.
  4. div:HTML container for grouping and styling content sections flexibly.
  5. ol: HTML tag for creating numbered lists sequentially.
  6. ul:HTML tag for creating bulleted lists of items.
